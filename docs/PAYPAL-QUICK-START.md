## 📥 When Someone Donates via PayPal

### You'll Receive This:
📧 **Email from PayPal** saying "You've got money!"

### What to Do (Takes 2 Minutes):

1. **Open the PayPal email**
   - Look for: Amount (in USD), Donor Name, Transaction ID

2. **Convert USD to DOP**
   - Example: $50 USD received
   - Check exchange rate (Google "USD to DOP" or use PayPal's conversion)
   - Today's rate: ~57 DOP per USD (example)
   - Calculate: $50 × 57 = RD$2,850

3. **Log into your Church Admin Panel**
   - Go to: Administration > Donations tab

4. **Click "Add Donation" button**

5. **Fill in these fields:**
   - Date: Today (or date from email)
   - Amount: **Enter in DOP** (example: 2850)
   - Category: General Fund/Tithes (or ask donor)
   - Payment Method: Select "PayPal"
   - Type: One-time
   - Donor Name: From PayPal email
   - Donor Email: From PayPal email
   - Transaction ID: **COPY THIS FROM PAYPAL EMAIL** (looks like: 8LK123456789ABCDE)
   - Notes: "Received $50 USD (rate: 57)"
   - Status: Completed

6. **Click "Save Donation"** ✅ Done!

## 📞 Common Questions

**Q: What if I miss recording a donation?**  
A: Check your PayPal Activity history. All past donations are there with Transaction IDs.

**Q: Can people donate if they don't have PayPal?**  
A: Yes! PayPal allows credit/debit card payments without an account.

**Q: What are the PayPal fees?**  
A: Usually 2.9% - 4.4% per transaction. Example: RD$1,000 donation = ~RD$30-45 fee.

**Q: How do I get money from PayPal to bank?**  
A: Log into PayPal > Transfer Money > Transfer to Bank. Takes 1-3 days.

**Q: What if someone donates in USD?**  
A: PayPal converts to DOP automatically. You receive DOP.
