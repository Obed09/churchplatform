## 💰 Cost Analysis

### PayPal Business Account Pricing

#### Option 1: Standard Business (Most Common)
**Cost:** FREE monthly fee
**Transaction Fees:**
- Domestic: 2.99% + $0.49 per transaction
- International: 4.49% + fixed fee
- PayPal Giving Fund: 0% (PayPal covers it!)

**Example:**
- $100 donation = $2.99 + $0.49 = **$3.48 fee** (you receive $96.52)
- RD$5,000 donation = RD$149.50 + RD$24.50 = **RD$174 fee** (you receive RD$4,826)

#### Option 2: PayPal Payments Pro
**Cost:** $30/month + transaction fees
**Benefits:**
- Accept credit cards without PayPal account
- Customers stay on your website
- More professional checkout
- Good for churches with >$10,000/month in donations

#### Option 3: PayPal Nonprofit Account ⭐ **RECOMMENDED**
**Requirements:** 501(c)(3) status or local nonprofit registration
**Cost:** FREE monthly fee
**Transaction Fees:** **1.99% + $0.49** (30% lower than standard!)

**Example:**
- $100 donation = $1.99 + $0.49 = **$2.48 fee** (you receive $97.52)
- Saves $1.00 per $100 vs Standard Business

**To Qualify:**
- Register church as nonprofit in Dominican Republic
- Provide registration documents to PayPal
- Approval takes 1-2 weeks
