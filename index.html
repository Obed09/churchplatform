For over 50 years, Tabernacle Amour Pour Le Calvaire - Assemblee De Dieu has been a beacon of hope and faith in our community. We are a diverse, multi-generational family united by our love for Christ and our commitment to serving others.

Our mission is to glorify God by making disciples who worship passionately, love sacrificially, and serve joyfully. We believe in the transformative power of the Gospel and strive to create an environment where everyone can encounter God's love.
