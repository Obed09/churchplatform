How to Submit a Finance Request

  1. Fill out the form completely - Provide all required information
  2. Attach supporting documents - Include quotes, invoices, or receipts if available
  3. Submit your request - Your request will be automatically routed for approval
  4. Track your request - You'll receive notifications at each stage
  5. Receive payment - Approved payments will be sent to your Billet account

Average approval time: 2-5 business days
